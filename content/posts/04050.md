`xmlstarlet` is a great tool for grabbing content out of XML.

	hendry@x61 1.1$ xmlstarlet sel -t -m '//Operation' -v '@identifier' -n test.xml
	onPropertyChange
	listVocabularies
	setDefaultVocabulary
	listAspects
	getComponents
	listProperties
	getPropertyValue
	setPropertyValue
	watchPropertyChange
	clearPropertyChange
