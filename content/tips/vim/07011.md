title: Editing two files
---

I usually

	CTRL+w N
to create a new window. and then:

	:edit otherfile
I use CTRL+w w to switch windows.
Also

	ctrl+w _ or = to
resize the window to max and equal partioning.
My friend uses

	:split otherfile
Which turns the first two steps into one.
