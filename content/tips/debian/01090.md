For the "Brazilian guy in Portugal" case referred to before,
this is where LC_* come in; you would set

	LC_MESSAGES=pt_BR

	LANG=pt_BR
to get Brazilian dialect of Portuguese, and everything else

	LC_MONETARY=pt
etc.
